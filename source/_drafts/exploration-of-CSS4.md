## CSS選擇器(selector)
在CSS中，選擇器分成四種表現方式。
> The term selector can refer to a simple selector, compound selector, complex selector, or selector list.

## :not()的連用
在CSS3規範裡，`:not()`裡頭的參數只能採用
``` css
button:not(disabled)
```
